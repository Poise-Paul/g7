G7 Patriots International

The relevance of youth to the development and economic growth of the nation can not be underrated. Youths are the driving force of any nation.

They are recognized and respected as social actors who constitute.

  • Value possession of any nation
  • Centre of reconstruction and development
  • A measure of capability of a country to reproduce and sustain itself
  • Future leaders in all sectors of society

G7 believes in harnessing the vibrance, adventurous minds, creative and risk prone nature of the youth towards a given set of ideology, creating a patriotic spirit.

Nigeria Our Home

Nigeria is a country with an estimated population of 200 million people, 54% of whom according to the National Bureau of Statistics are youths. Of this number, only a tiny fraction are currently engaging the society constructively, either in society's own terms (gainfully employed) or on their own terms (private individuals creating employment and wealth). The large majority are unemployed, under employed, frustrated, voiceless, dependent, despondent and disenchanted with the system. Some others exist on the marginal periphery of society surviving by engaging in anti social activities, ranging from drug use and trafficking, to cultism and other vices. The result is that many young Nigerians do not feel sufficiently persuaded about a stake in the Nigerian dream or project. They therefore cannot relate with society in a manner to unleash their patriotic spirit and zeal. Several interventionist policies and programmes (notable the National Youth Policy documents of 2009) are currently in place to address the several challenges that confront majority of young Nigerians. G7 objective is to complement existing interventions, build on them and foster youth involvement in positive contributions to society through the pursuit and implementation of seven (7) cardinal goals (G7) that are crucial in making young Nigerians true patriots.
