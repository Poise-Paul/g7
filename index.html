Who We Are

We are a non-profit, non-partisan, non-governmental organization aimed at revitalizing the patriotic spirit and zeal in young Nigerians through passion for God and Country. Each human individual is born, brought up and gain a given grip on life by a particular set of communities, which almost invariably includes one of a diversity of nations. G7 believe in one ideology, and that is the love for home land with a readiness to make self sacrifices to bring out the best out of Nigerians and Nigeria as a whole. Though tribes and tongues may differ, Nigeria is our nation to be build through love, patriotism and sense of belonging.

The seven cardinal goals of the G7 Patriots International are as follows:

1

Civic Duties

Inculcate knowledge of respect for national symbols, flag, anthem, pledge, holidays, heroes, former leaders, institutions etc. Volunteering.

2

Career Counseling

Provide counseling on career and study choices on a clear understanding of the labour market and emerging job opportunities. Providing meaningful information in which they can base decisions relating to further education and training. Provide career information guidance through collaborative efforts between educational institutions, business and other labor organizations.

3

Citizens Rights & Obligations

Foster understanding of their rights as citizens of Nigeria as enunciated in chapter four of the 1999 constitution (as amended). Inculcate a sense of responsibility through elaboration of their obligations to the Nigerian state and society. Advocacy of various causes such as political, economic, civil, environmental, etc.

4

Value Re-Orientation/Ethics

Moral development and value orientation. Propagating observance of the principles of transparency, accountability, hard work, diligence, non-discrimination, mutual assistance, self restraint, self respect, sense of identity, creativity, truth, honor, tolerance, justice, freedom, equality, diversity, human rights, respect for authority and rule of law etc.

5

Leadership Development

Sensitizing and inculcating the leadership traits of: Vision , Drive and Determination, Cognitive Capacity, Self Confidence, Integrity and Sociability.

6

Financial Independence

Support for enterprise projects, access to microfinance, cooperative schemes in production and marketing of goods and services, recognition of self employment as a legitimate and meaningful career option and not just an option of last resorts, savings and investment practices and options.

7

Political Participation

Promote active participation of young men and women in the political process at local government, state and national levels. Grassroots mobilization behind preferred candidates. Voting and voter education. Promote understanding of electoral laws. Articulate, advertise and communicate demands across issue areas to policy makers in the executive and legislative arms of government.

Our Partners

In pursuit of it's goals and objectives and in recognition and appreciation of the intervention measures already in the field, in particular; the National Youth Policy, The National Economic and Development Strategy (NEEDS), The Transformation Agenda, The Millennium Goals, The ECOWAS Commission Youth Strategy and The African Youth Charter, G7 Patriots International will establish cooperative interaction with all stakeholders including: The different tiers of government, private sector and civil society organizations and international development partners.

Final Word

G7 is committed to the welfare and improvement of society, it's people, it's culture, and future through the mobilization of young Nigerians around the principles and visions of G7 Patriots International. Every Nigerian child should have a chance to grow up and aspire, if so inclined to the highest office in our land. Our believe in the sacrifice of personal interest to public welfare is very essential. G7 also believes in a sense of collective responsibility because saying you are a patriot is not enough; you have to be one from the heart.
